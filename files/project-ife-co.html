Overview

COLOR AS IDENTITY

Brand Identity Social Media Visual System Instagram

Ife+Co is a multi-disciplinary company offering services across real estate, web development, project management, product testing, graphic design, and architectural services.

With that many service categories, a visually inconsistent feed would fragment the brand's identity. Rather than assigning unrelated visual themes to each service, the system was built around a more strategic principle — using the brand's core colors as the primary recognition device across every category.

This approach ensures that every post, regardless of service type, immediately reinforces the company's identity.

The Result

DISCIPLINED REPETITION AS A BRAND ASSET

The design avoids visual noise and instead uses disciplined repetition as a branding asset. Across six distinct service categories, the identity remains immediately recognizable.

The message becomes clear: the brand is recognizable even before the logo is noticed. Color does the work. Consistency builds trust. And the audience learns to associate the palette with the company long before they read the content.
